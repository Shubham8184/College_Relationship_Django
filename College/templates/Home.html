We are Hindustan Aeronautical Institute



Hindustan Aeronautical Institute is an offshoot of Hindustan Aeronautical Institute Technologies which has been in siness for over 20 years in Nigeria and Ghana. The company is created to cater for the books need of Africans living in USA as well as those living back home. Putting our wealth of business experience in use and with our network of companies in Nigeria and Ghana, Hindustan Aeronautical Institute is setup in 2018 to try to bridge the gap between the two continents in respect of movement of goods between both continents.

Often times when we hear books, movement of goods generally come to Mind, not far from the truth. However, books does not only entail movement of goods but also the procurement of such goods. Therefore, Hindustan Aeronautical Institute receives specific requests and orders for products from people back in Africa who are not able to make purchases internationally or for one reason or the other are limited in their reach to outside world, then source for the specific item, buy the product and have it shipped to the buyer as quickly as possible.

Amongst our services too, Speed would also be serving our African families and surprisingly, the increasing number of foreigners who may be interested in African cuisine and delicacies, our renowned “Soup Packs” which are designed to bring our Original “African Soup” flavors here to Africans and other nationals. Our Vacuum Sealed components of the Soup Packs are packaged to retain their original favors for over a long period of time without the use of any preservatives or chemicals. Of course Africans take pride in eating purely Organic Food, and we make sure that we stick to that principle.
